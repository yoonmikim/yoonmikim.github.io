Intro

Hello! Welcome to Yoonmi's Personal Website for Portfolio. By the way, don't forget to check out my awesome work.

Hi, my name is Yoonmi Kim. I’m an US Citizen, moved from South Korea 10 years ago. Bilingual with fluent english & korean, and sufficient chinese. Graduated from Rutgers University in May 2018, with a degree of Biological Sciences, on pre-med track, I took take a gap year to go med school, but I found out that I did not want to become a doctor anymore. Thus, I reevaluated myself and did research to discover programming field. As a technophile, I self-taught HTML and CSS, and then I discovered App Academy's Jumpstart Program and Bootcamp Prep, I instantly fell in love with coding, which led me to Flatiron School’s bootcamp. Now as an Asian American female Full Stack Web Developer with a passion for AI, VR, and future technology, I think outside of box and seek to make a mark in technology by making applications to improve people's lives. Believing in lifelong learning, I am eager to find solutions to difficult problems and to build a meaningful community. With education in medicine and research, I bring strong skills in pair programming, teamwork, data analysis skills in research, cross-functional collaboration, project management that help doctor-patient-nurse at GIM in Robert Wood Johnson Medical School, and I also learned genuine compassion and intercommunication skills from tutoring my students.

As a technophile, travelholic, wanderlust myself, I fell in love with coding. Coding is amazing in a way that its intricate, cutting-edge, trendy, always ever-changing nature is truly exciting, and similar to medicine, the complexity and lifelong learning go all the way in life. Both fields strive to help improve human life, which I love the most about. By employing the analytical skills that I've developed in the past as a premed, biology student, as well as the coding skills obtained from my professional and educational experiences, I am looking to help build fully-functioning applications that can help human life better, more convenient, and efficient.

Transitioned to coding to help data-driven companies tell their stories intuitively and meaningfully. With a passion and curiosity for solving problems through statistical data analysis in research, I bring strong analytical skills in mining/interpreting data, cross-functional collaboration, and project-management that help teams of any size work efficiently and solidify best practices with the compelling desire to build projects that create impact in society.
